## Motivation
* The field of adversarial machine learning has expanded rapidly, leading to a fragmented landscape of disparate attacks and defenses.
* Researchers and practitioners struggle to keep pace with the sheer volume of literature and the evolving taxonomy of threats.
* A comprehensive systematization of knowledge is required to bridge the gap between theoretical vulnerabilities and practical security.

## Contribution
* Provides an exhaustive taxonomy and categorization of the latest adversarial attacks and defensive methodologies.
* Evaluates the strengths and weaknesses of current paradigms, offering comparative analyses of state-of-the-art techniques.
* Outlines critical open challenges and future research directions for the AI security community.

## Summary
This survey provides a comprehensive overview of the adversarial machine learning landscape, systematically categorizing recent advancements in both attack and defense strategies to serve as a foundational reference for future research.
