# 3. Towards Evaluating the Robustness of Neural Networks (Carlini & Wagner, 2017)

## 🤔 Why You Must Read This Paper
In 2016 and 2017, researchers proposed dozens of 'defenses' (like defensive distillation) that claimed to solve adversarial attacks. Carlini and Wagner stepped in and completely dismantled almost all of them. Reading this paper is crucial for understanding how to rigorously evaluate a defense instead of just throwing a weak attack at it and claiming victory.

## 💡 What It Contributed to the Field
It introduced the C&W attack, an optimization-based attack that is mathematically elegant and incredibly powerful. For years, the C&W attack stood as the gold-standard benchmark. If a proposed defense couldn't survive C&W, it wasn't a real defense.
